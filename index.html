Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives a country's growth, fosters technological advancement, improves living standards, and strengthens global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balanced economic growth, environmental preservation, and social equity for a nation's future.

Infrastructure

A country's infrastructure includes transportation, energy, communication, healthcare, education, water supply, and technological advancements.

Empowerment

Empowerment of a country involves education, innovation, equality, strong leadership, economic growth, and social development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh is a land of endless possibilities, where even the poorest can achieve extraordinary things. We can create a Bangladesh where poverty will be a term confined to history books. We saw the power of small loans and entrepreneurship transforming lives and lifting families out of poverty. Empowering women in Bangladesh has proven that they are the real change-makers in their communities.Bangladesh can lead the way in demonstrating how economic development and environmental sustainability go hand in hand.Let’s imagine a Bangladesh where everyone has equal opportunities to thrive and succeed.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
